READMERaw rodent and rainfall data from the study site at Las Chinchillas National Reserve near the city of Illapel, Coquimbo Region of Chile (31М50е87ее S, 71М10е53ее W).Columns areYearPD = Phyllotis darwini minimun number alivePDD = Phyllotis darwini density (minimun number alive/trapping area)XPD = natural logarithm of P. darwini densityAO = Abrothrix olivacea minimun number aliveAOD = Abrothrix olivacea density (minimun number alive/trapping area)XAO = natural logarithm of Abrothrix olivacea densityRPD = logarithmic (per capita) reproductive rate of Phyllotis darwiniRAO = logarithmic (per capita) reproductive rate of Abrothrix olivaceaP = Annual accumulated rainfall (mm)P1 = one year lagged annual accumulated rainfall (mm)
